Welcome to RouterFleet!

Hello! Welcome to RouterFleet, an open source project designed to simplify router backups. We currently support Mikrotik and OpenWRT devices, and we're excited to expand this list soon.

If you have a specific device you would like supported or suggestions for improvement, please visit our GitHub page to share your thoughts. Your contributions help us enhance and evolve RouterFleet for all users.

Thank you for your support and involvement in making RouterFleet better for everyone!
